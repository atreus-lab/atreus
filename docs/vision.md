# Vision: ZK-PayLink

## Mission
To build the privacy-preserving payment distribution infrastructure for the Stellar ecosystem, enabling secure, walletless, and private asset transfers for everyone, everywhere.

## Problem Statement
Traditional blockchain payments suffer from two primary friction points:
1.  **Onboarding Friction**: Recipients must have a wallet and public key *before* they can receive funds.
2.  **Privacy Leakage**: Claiming funds via public links (like standard "PayLinks") often reveals the link secret or the recipient's identity on-chain, creating a trail that links the sender, the link, and the ultimate recipient in plain view.

## Why Payment Distribution Privacy Matters
For retail users, privacy is a safety feature. For businesses, it is a compliance and competitive necessity. 
- **Payroll**: Distributing wages via links shouldn't expose individual employee addresses or amounts to the public.
- **Grants/Gifts**: Senders should be able to distribute funds without creating a public map of their social or professional network.
- **Disbursements**: Humanitarian or insurance disbursements require high privacy to protect vulnerable recipients.

## Why Onboarding Matters
Web3 will never reach the next billion users if the first step is "Download a wallet and write down 24 words." ZK-PayLink allows the *claim* to be the onboarding event. By using Passkeys (WebAuthn), we turn every smartphone into a secure hardware wallet without the user even knowing they are using crypto.

## Long-Term Vision
ZK-PayLink aims to become the "Stripe for Private Distribution" on Stellar. We envision a future where any Stellar-based application can use our SDK to send private, link-based payments as easily as sending a WhatsApp message, backed by the mathematical certainty of Zero-Knowledge Proofs and the hardware security of modern mobile devices.
